Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about using NotesRepo

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, choose whether you're a student or teacher, and fill in your details. Once you verify your email, you'll be ready to start using NotesRepo.

What file formats can I upload?

NotesRepo supports a wide range of file formats including PDF, DOCX, PPT, TXT, images (JPG, PNG), and more. If you're on the free plan, you can upload PDF and TXT files. Student and Teacher plans support all available formats.

How do I share my notes with specific people?

When uploading or editing your notes, you can set privacy options. Choose "Public" to share with everyone, "Private" to keep them just for yourself, or "Specific Users/Groups" to share with selected classmates or classes. You can also generate shareable links with optional passwords.

Is there a mobile app available?

While we don't have a dedicated mobile app yet, our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. You can access all features through your mobile browser. A dedicated app is in our development roadmap for the future.

How can teachers use NotesRepo in their classroom?

Teachers can create class groups where they can share materials with students, track who has accessed resources, create assignments, and facilitate discussions. The Teacher Plan includes analytics to see which materials are most used and helpful to students.
